Anthony Roytman is an emerging playwright and software developer. He currently works as a Solutions Engineering Analyst at Deloitte Consulting and recently graduated from Northwestern University in June 2021 with a BS in Theatre and Computer Science.


His ten-minute play Filthy Animals was produced in Vertigo Production's 2021 Ten Minute Play Festival, and his drama Bigger Than I Thought received the 2020 Agnes Nixon Play Award and was read in the 2020 Agnes Nixon Playwriting Festival. He served as a board member on Vertigo Productions, a Northwestern University student theatre board dedicated to producing student-created new work; as their Co-Production Manager (2020-21) he advised production teams working with Vertigo Productions, and as Special Events Chair (2019-20) he produced new music and new comedy events.


He writes about technology, the natural world, the wisdom of animals, and the folly of humans. His favorite album to listen to while writing is Wake Up Calls by Cosmo Sheldrake.
